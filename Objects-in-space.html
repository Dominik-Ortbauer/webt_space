Objects in space

Space is full of stuff... well, kind of. As already mentioned it is mostly empty space, but if you add it up there is a lot of stuff. To be exact: Stars, Dust and other solid objects.

There are different kinds of Stars like brown dwarfs, red dwarfs, neutron stars, normal Stars, red giants and after they die, sometimes they explode and become black holes. These explosions are called supernovas and they create a lot of Dust from which new planets and asteroids arise.

Probably the most well known Star is our sun. It is the only star that provides energy to intelligent life. At least as far as we know.It has nine Planets circeling it, one of which is earth.
