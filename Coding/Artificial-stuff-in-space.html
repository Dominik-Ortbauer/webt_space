Artificial stuff in Space

Even though it's hard and expensive to get to space, humanity was able to do it. And we haven't stopped. Each year there are still tens of satellites put into low earth orbit. And this this number will only get higher. Elon Musk is planning to send 60,000 Satellites in low orbit which will bring an internet connection to everyone. But the problem with that is that the probability of two satellites colliding will rise exponetial.

When two Satellites collide with each other they shatter. Parts of shattered Satellites are calles Space Debris. Space Debris can be categorised as following.

Size Number of fragments
1 - 10mm 50,000,000
10 - 100mm 300,000
>100mm 12,000

The first satellite was put in orbit in 1957. Since then the first human on the moon in 1969 and the first space station in 1971.
